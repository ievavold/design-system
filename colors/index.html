Accessibility

The colors in this design system must meet WCAG 2.1 Level AA guidelines (a contrast ratio of at least 4.5:1) for 14px to 16px text/icons.

All color samples are accompanied by a foreground color that meets or exceeds WCAG 2.1 Level AA guidelines.

Foreground colors are used for text and icons. Make sure when selecting a foreground color that it has enough contrast with its background. Identity and utility color samples indicate the appropriate foreground color to use.
